## 🔴 Business Problem  

Flight ticket prices fluctuate based on **seasonality, airline, duration, and demand**.  
Without predictive analysis:  
- ❌ Travelers can’t plan budgets effectively  
- ❌ Airlines miss opportunities for dynamic pricing  
- ❌ Travel agencies can’t recommend optimal booking times  

➡️ The goal is to use **machine learning** to predict flight prices and uncover **key factors influencing airfare**.

## ⚙️ Action (What I Did)

╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗

- 🧹 **Data Cleaning** → Removed nulls, standardized airline/route names, fixed data types  
- 📊 **Exploratory Analysis** → Price distribution, airline vs price, duration vs price  
- ✈️ **Feature Engineering** → One‑hot encoding for airlines/routes, extracted departure hour, binned duration  
- 🤖 **ML Modeling** → Built models (Linear Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost) to predict ticket prices  
- 📈 **Visualization** → Feature importance plots, correlation heatmaps, boxplots  

---

## 📈 Results  

- ✅ **Airline Impact** → Premium airlines had consistently higher fares  
- ✅ **Duration Effect** → Longer flights correlated with higher ticket prices  
- ✅ **Departure Time** → Evening flights were more expensive than morning flights  
- ✅ **Model Accuracy** → Random Forest achieved ~88% accuracy in price prediction  
- ✅ **Feature Importance** → Airline, route, and duration were top predictors
